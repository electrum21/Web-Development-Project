Oh My God

(G)I-DLE promoted their title track 'Oh My God' for 3 weeks in April 2020. It was released in both Korean and English through Cube Entertainment on April 6, 2020. The song was composed by Soyeon and long-time collaborator Yummy Tone, while the lyrics for the English version were translated by Lauren Kaori.

Luv U

(G)I-DLE performed this track in their newest album 'I Trust' to thank their fans for their support during this period. They asked fans to vote among 5 choices for the outfit of their last performance, and the winning choice was the animal onesies seen in the above video.

Uh-Oh

'Uh-Oh' was released on June 26, 2019, by Cube Entertainment as (G)I-DLE's digital single. Uh-Oh ranked number 22 on NetEase Cloud Music China for the first half of 2019. The choreography also reflects the rebellious vibe of the song with lots of powerful moves accompanied with tough expressions.

Senorita

'Senorita' was released on February 26, 2019, as the title track from the group's second EP I Made (2019). Billboard described 'Senorita' as a "Latin pop-themed dance track that incorporates castanets and jazzy brass horns alongside groovy rhythmic strings and sleek electronic effects".
